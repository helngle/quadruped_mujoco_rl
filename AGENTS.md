# Project Maintenance

- Keep `docs/PROJECT_JOURNAL.md` current after meaningful environment, training, evaluation,
  checkpoint, or roadmap changes.
- Record both successful and failed experiments. Do not rewrite earlier outcomes to match later
  conclusions.
- Mark active training as in progress. Add quantitative conclusions only after evaluating the
  resulting checkpoint.
- Update the journal's current status, relevant version section, and update log in the same change.
